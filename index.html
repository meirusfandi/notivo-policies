Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Notivo is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data Only: All your reminders, categories, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Information: We don't collect names, emails, phone numbers, or personal identifiers
  • No Account Required: The app works without creating an account or signing in
  • Device Information: Basic device info for app functionality and crash reporting

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core reminder and notification functionality
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Ensure notifications work properly on your device

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored locally using Hive database
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device
  • Secure: Data is encrypted and protected by your device's security
  • No Backups: We don't backup your data to external servers

4. Permissions

Notivo requests minimal permissions:

  • Notifications: To send reminder alerts
  • Storage: To save your reminders locally

5. Third-Party Services

Notivo uses:

  • Firebase Analytics: Basic usage analytics (anonymous)
  • Crashlytics: Crash reporting to improve app stability

6. Children's Privacy

Notivo is safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with the updated date.
